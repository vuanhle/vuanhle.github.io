# Venture Capital

<p class="lead">I am interested in venture capital as a way to compound technical insight into company formation, research translation, and long-term frontier infrastructure.</p>

## Target Roles

- Deep-tech VC analyst, associate, fellow, or scout roles.
- AI infrastructure and AI4Science investment research.
- Technical diligence for frontier AI, scientific software, geospatial intelligence, and computational science companies.
- Venture studio or company-building roles where research judgment shapes product and market direction.

## Investment Themes

- **AI reliability infrastructure:** evaluation, contamination detection, monitoring, governance, and systems that make frontier models trustworthy.
- **AI for science:** protein design, scientific foundation models, lab automation, simulation, and constrained generative systems.
- **Geospatial and climate intelligence:** satellite data, infrastructure risk, urban resilience, and spatial decision systems.
- **Research-to-market tooling:** platforms that help technical teams turn scientific insight into repeatable workflows.

## Diligence Lens

I like companies where technical depth creates defensibility: proprietary data loops, hard-to-replicate research insight, strong engineering velocity, and a clear wedge into a painful market. My research background helps me read the technical substrate; my venture interest pushes me to ask whether that substrate can become a business.
